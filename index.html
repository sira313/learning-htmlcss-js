About Aflasio

At the beginning, Aflasio is just an acronym of “Argia Flat Design Motion” because i was learning about graphic motion that time. But now, i’m just doing digital painting artwork. The only reason that i’m not change that name is because i love it and easy to find on google.

About Author

My name is Sira Argia. I come from Indonesia, exactly at Sanggau regency, West Kalimantan. I’m a FLOSS user artist and this blog is about my portfolio. I use KDE Neon as a single OS on my laptop. The softwares that i use for work is:
- Krita for digital painting
- Inkscape for the vector artwork
- Gimp for edit the photos
